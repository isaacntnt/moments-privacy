moments. — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

moments ("we", "our", "the app") is a personal journaling app built for capturing meaningful life moments. Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What We Collect

  • Email address — used solely for account creation and sign-in.
  • Moment entries — the text you write in response to prompts (what happened, why it mattered, what to remember) and any metadata such as timestamps, prompt set used, and favorite status.
  • Photos — images you optionally attach to a moment, stored securely in cloud storage linked to your account.
  • App preferences — theme choice, notification settings, and default prompt set, stored locally on your device.

How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the core app experience: saving, displaying, and searching your moments.
  • To authenticate you and keep your data private.
  • To send you optional daily reminder notifications (only if you enable them).

We do not use your data for advertising, analytics, profiling, or training AI models.

Where Your Data Is Stored

Your account and moment data are stored on Supabase, a cloud database provider. Photos are stored in Supabase Storage. All data is transmitted over HTTPS and access is restricted to your account through row-level security policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose your personal data to third parties. Your moments are yours alone.

Data Export and Deletion

  • Export: You can export all your moment data as a JSON file at any time from the Settings screen.
  • Deletion: You can delete individual moments within the app. To delete your entire account and all associated data, contact us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

moments is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
